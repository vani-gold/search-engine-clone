About

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro GOIH ComM is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward and captains the Portugal national team. He is currently a free agent. Wikipedia

Born: February 5, 1985 (age 37 years), Hospital Dr. Nélio Mendonça, Funchal, Portugal

Children: Cristiano Ronaldo Jr., Alana Martina dos Santos Aveiro, Bella Esmeralda, Mateo Ronaldo, Eva Maria Dos Santos Current team: Portugal national football team (#7 / Forward)
